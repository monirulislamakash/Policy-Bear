ACA Marketplace Plans

What Are ACA Plans?

Affordable Care Act (ACA) plans are comprehensive health insurance policies that cover essential health benefits and cannot deny coverage based on pre-existing conditions.

  • No denial for pre-existing conditions
  • Essential health benefits covered
  • Preventive care at no cost
  • Subsidies available based on income

Plan Types Available

HMO Plans

Lower costs, network restrictions

PPO Plans

More flexibility, higher costs

EPO Plans

Network-based, no referrals needed

POS Plans

Hybrid of HMO and PPO
